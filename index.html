Our Network

HealthScope Network connects people across the country through honest stories and shared experiences. We've grown into a national community reaching millions of listeners, readers, and viewers every month — all united by a simple goal: to make health feel human again. Today, our reach spans all 50 states, with a growing audience of over 250,000 active subscribers and more than 10 million monthly impressions across podcasts, video, and digital platforms. Our network brings together patients, caregivers, advocates, and healthcare voices who believe that information only matters when it connects to real life. We collaborate with trusted health organizations, research groups, and community advocates to share accurate, accessible, and human-centered stories that make a difference.
